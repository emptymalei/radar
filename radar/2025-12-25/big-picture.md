---
title: "Hold on to the Big Picture"
ring: adopt
quadrant: methods-and-philosophies
tags: [life, thinking]
---

Cling to the big picture in everything you do. When faced with decisions, challenges, or distractions, always go back to review the big picture.

Think about your long-term goals, values, and vision. Don't be scared away from complexity or difficulty.

In the era of AI, this is especially important.
